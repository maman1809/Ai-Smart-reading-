🎯 Our Mission

No one should waste time Googling every unknown word. SmartReader brings the dictionary, grammar guide, and translator directly into your reading — in one tap.

💡 Why We Built This

As an English educator, I saw students constantly interrupting their reading to look up words. SmartReader keeps them focused and learning naturally.

📚 What It Does

Upload any PDF, TXT, or DOCX. Tap any word for its meaning, part of speech, and translation. Switch to Sentence Mode for grammar analysis.

🌍 Who It's For

Students improving English, teachers preparing materials, professionals reading documents, and anyone learning through reading.
